About Me

I love making things... because why not!

My name is Bryan, and for as long as I can remember I have loved all kinds of tech - Commodore computers, IOT toys, web services, you name it. To keep my love alive I obtained a bachelor's degree from The University of Texas at Austin in computer science with a minor in sociology. Following UT Austin I began working as a software developer at Salesforce.com. In my free time I try to answer the calls to my curiosity in any way I can, even if what I learn or create doesn't have an immediate practical use...the experience more times than not proves to be beneficial! This site is an excuse to practice my web development skills, as well as keep track of the things that make me who I am.
